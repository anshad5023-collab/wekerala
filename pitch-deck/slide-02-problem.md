## SPEAKER NOTES (what you say out loud)
```
"Small shop owners today face 3 specific problems.

First — they want to automate WhatsApp orders,
but every solution requires technical setup they can't do.

Second — there's no AI working for them.
Nobody is predicting when rice or oil will run out.
Nobody is reminding a customer who hasn't ordered in 2 weeks.

Third — even if they try different tools,
nothing connects. Website is one app, billing is another,
WhatsApp is another. They end up managing 4 logins
and nothing talks to each other.

That's the gap ORTAS fills."
```
Time: 45–60 seconds.
